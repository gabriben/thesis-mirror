\section*{Appendix}
\label{section:hyperparameters}
Here we detail the FOCUS hyperparameters across the \numprint{42} settings in Experiments 1 and 2. $\sigma$ indicates the steepness of the sigmoid function in Equation~\ref{eq:sigma}; $\tau$ is the temperature of the softmax in Equation~\ref{eq:tau}; $\beta$ is the trade-off parameter in Equation~\ref{eq:approxloss}; $\alpha$ is the learning rate of Adam.
